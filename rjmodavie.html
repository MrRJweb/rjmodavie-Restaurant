Our Story

A table where time slows down

Born from a small family kitchen and a stubborn love of good produce, RJ-Modavie has grown into a neighbourhood institution. Every evening our chefs write the menu around what the morning market offered — nothing more, nothing less.

We believe hospitality is a craft: warm bread on the table within minutes, wine poured with a story, and desserts that arrive just as the candles burn low. Pull up a chair; the night is yours.

— The Modavie Family
